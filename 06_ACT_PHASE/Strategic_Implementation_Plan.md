# Strategic Implementation & Measurement Plan

## Phase 1: Short-Term Actions (0-3 Months)
* **Action:** Design and approve the visual assets for the "Summer Conversion Campaign."
* **Action:** Set up geo-fenced digital advertising perimeters around the top 5 Casual Rider stations identified in the geographic analysis.
* **KPI (Key Performance Indicator) to Track:** Click-through rate (CTR) on digital ads placed near the lakefront stations.

## Phase 2: Mid-Term Actions (3-6 Months)
* **Action:** Launch the "Weekend Explorer" upgrade promotion in the Cyclistic mobile app.
* **Action:** Send targeted push notifications to casual users who exceed 20 minutes on a single weekend ride, offering a discount on an annual pass.
* **KPI to Track:** The conversion rate of users who upgrade to a membership within 24 hours of a weekend ride.

## Future Data Considerations for Next Year
To refine this analysis in the future, the data team recommends:
1. Implementing anonymized user IDs to track if a specific casual rider actually converts to a member over time.
2. Integrating daily weather data to measure exactly how temperature drops impact the casual ridership baseline.
